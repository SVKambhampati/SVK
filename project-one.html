EnvisionVR

Overview

EnvisionVR is a virtual reality education platform developed by my team and me to assist special‐needs students who struggle with visual‐spatial reasoning. The application delivers interactive 3D environments that enhance cognitive development and conceptual understanding through immersive, hands‐on learning experiences across a range of subjects.

Key Subject Modules

  • Biology: A guided human anatomy module where students can explore detailed skeletal and organ systems through interactive modeling.
  • Geography: An explorable Earth globe with dynamic topographical maps and real‐time environmental simulations.
  • Physics: A virtual physics lab for conducting experiments like free‐fall kinematics with live motion tracking and visualizations.
  • Astronomy: A 3D solar system where students can interact with rotating planets, moons, and orbits to learn about space in an engaging way.

Technologies

We used Unity, Meta SDKs, and C# to design the platform, emphasizing intuitive interfaces and immersive environments.

  • Custom 3D modeling and shader development for realism.
  • Real‐time motion tracking and dynamic simulation for interactivity.
  • Performance optimization for smooth rendering and scene transitions.
  • Object‐oriented programming for modular development and stability.
  • Integration of physics engines, animation controllers, and event‐driven scripting to simulate real‐world phenomena.

Impact

This project demonstrates how technology can be harnessed to bridge educational gaps, offering transformative tools that make learning more engaging and accessible for all students.
